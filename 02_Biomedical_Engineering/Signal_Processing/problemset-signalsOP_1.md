### P1.1
Evaluate each of the following expressions for the complex number $z = \frac{1}{2} e^{j\frac{\pi}{4}}$. 

(a) $\Re{(z)}$

$$
Evaluation: \quad 
\Re (\frac{1}{2} (cos \theta + isin \theta)) = \frac{1}{2}cos\theta
$$

(b) $\Im{(z)}$

$$ \Im (\frac{1}{2} (cos \theta + isin \theta)) = \frac{1}{2}isin\theta $$

(c) $|z|$ 
by definition
$$
|z| = \frac{1}{2}
$$

(d) $\sphericalangle z$ 

$$
\sphericalangle z = \frac{\pi}{4} 
$$

(e) $z*(* denotes complex conjugation)$ 
The complex conjugation of $z$ is

$$
\frac{1}{2}(cos\theta - isin\theta)
$$
its also has the representation of:
$$
\frac{1}{2}e^{-j\frac{\pi}{4}}
$$
(f) $z + z*$

$$
\frac{1}{2}(cos\theta + isin\theta) + \frac{1}{2}(cos\theta - isin\theta) = cos\theta  
$$
